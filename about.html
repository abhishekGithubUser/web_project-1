About KIT

In 1981 vibrant group of leading industrialists, professionals and educationalists established a trust: Kolhapur Institute of Technology popularly known as KIT.
In 1983 KIT started KIT's College of Engineering: The first among the self financed engineering colleges in Maharashtra. Created future of 16,000+ skilled engineers serving across the globe.
The second venture of KIT, The KIT's IMER is established in 1994, as recently celebrating its silver jubilee year.
The IMER has MBA program with 120 intake and MCA program with 60 intake. The institute is committed to provide quality education. So far, approximately 4000 students have completed their MBA and MCA program from IMER.
After successful results in Engineering and Management Institutions, this year KIT is starting 'KIT Academy' for the students of 11 & 12 Science. This academy will guide students professionally and systematically in their preparation of competitive exams like JEE, NEET and CET.
