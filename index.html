Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer Demo or Trial class before
joining?

Yes, this year our demo class will be commencing from 24th of May for seven days.

Are you providing any study material and mock tests, revision, crash courses?

Yes of course, the students will be provided the best study material of India and biweekly/triweekly all India test series where their regular performance growth will be measured.

What is the infrastructure and learning environment provided?

Smart classrooms for efficient teaching learning process. ICT enabled result oriented teaching-learning process. Well-equipped library with text books, ample reference books, question paper sets etc.

What’s the batch size? Will I get personal attention?

Batch size will be maximum 50 only. One to one student teacher interaction is committed. Regular doubt clearing sessions will be available at least of one to two hours a day.

How are you better than the other classes?

“History speaks for itself” – KIT has been setting the benchmark in the field of study of Engineering and MBA through their immense placement records every year for last 39 years of journey. We are committed to students and stakeholders for best results and following it successfully produced more than 15000 engineers getting campus placements and becoming entrepreneurs becoming one of the most prominent and successful Institute for Engineering Education in western Maharashtra. Now when we are on the verge of starting our academy we are coming up with tie-up with best organisations of India to provide profound platform to our students
- To inculcate their potential with the aid of best study material and
- To enhance their performance based on the biweekly or monthly all India test series.

What is the feedback structure?

Faculty feedback at regular intervals.

What’s the fee structure? Are there any discounts and what payment schemes are available?

Most affordable fee structure where easy instalment schemes will be provided.

Are the faculty members available besides classes?

Yes, always – our faculty members are always available beyond their regular class timing.
